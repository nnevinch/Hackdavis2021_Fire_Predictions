About

With accelerating climate change, the risk of wildfires has been growing every year. Increased global temperatures leads to earlier springs, which results in a rapid melting of snow that causes soils to stay dry for a longer period of time and creates the conditions for a fire. This effect of climate change has the potential to bring harm to people globally.

Our wildfire prediction model allows communities to better prepare for the risks of a fire in a world where they are growing in frequency and intensity.
